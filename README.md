## ⚔️ Weaponry & Armor
<details> 
  <summary> Spoiler </summary>

### 🛡️ Void Chainmail Set 🟣
* **Full Set Bonuses:**  **Slow Falling II:** Grants the Slow Falling II effect while the complete set is equipped.
  * **Void Rescue:** If you fall into the void (below Y -64), you are instantly teleported back to your last safe ground position.
* **Penalty:** Using the **Void Rescue** mechanic consumes **1/3** of the max durability of all four armor pieces.
* Includes Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, and Boots.

### 👣 Ender Boots 🟣
* Grants stealth by stopping vibration signals (Sculk sensors won't detect you).
* **Recipe:** Combine Leather Boots with Void Treated Leather at a Smithing Table.

### 🏹 Dragon Bow 🟣
* A powerful long-range weapon that shoots with **twice the velocity** of a vanilla bow and is **more precise**.

### 🗡 Relic Blade 🟣
* A sharp blade forged from 3 End Steel Ingots arranged in a vertical column.

### 🗡️ Relic Greatsword 🟣
* A massive sword with **8.5** Attack damage and a slow **0.6** Attack Speed.
* **Recipe:** Place a **Relic Blade** (top), a **Stick** (middle), and **Void Treated Leather** (bottom) in a vertical column.

### 🗡️ Soul Siphon 🔵
* Has the **same** Attack Damage as a **Stone Sword** but with a fast **2.6** Attack Speed.
* Entities killed by the **Soul Siphon** drop a **Soul Fragment**.
  </details>

## ⬛ Blocks
<details> 
  <summary> Spoiler </summary>

### 🧺 Sieve ⚪
* A functional block used for sifting materials like gravel and sand.

### 🔮 Void Pedestal 🟣
* Prevents teleportation within a **20-block radius** when active.
* **Activation:** Place an **Ender Eye** inside.

### ⚓ Void Anchor 🟣
* Used as an "anchor" for the **Static Pearl**.
* Can be dyed with **magenta dye** and washed with a water bucket.

### 🪚 Uncrafter ⚪
* Outputs a **random** ingredient from an item's crafting or smelting recipes.

### 🪨 Waystone ⚪
* A teleportation marker activated with **10 Soul Fragments**.
* Teleports the owner back if their health drops below 2.5 hearts.

### 🧲 Gravity Anchor ⚪
* Projects an energy field in a **10-block radius** that freezes falling blocks in mid-air when powered by Redstone.
  </details>

## Items
<details> 
  <summary> Spoiler </summary>

### 🧲 Magnet Ring ⚪
* **Range:** 4 blocks.
* **Modes:** Toggle between **Attracting** and **Repulsing** items by right-clicking.

### 🔘 Static Pearl 🟣
* Links to a Void Anchor to allow instant teleportation. Can be used **20 times**.

### 🏺 Foundation Pottery Sherd ⚪
* A unique sherd used for decorating pots with a custom pattern.

### 🕷️ Climbing Spider Leg ⚪
* Allows the player to climb walls while held in either hand.

### 📏 Measuring Tape ⚪
* Calculates **Vector** or **Manhattan** distance between two selected blocks.

### 🪣 Infinite Water Bucket ⚪
* Sources infinite water. **Shift + Right-Click** on fluids to drain them.

### 📐 Level (Architect's Level) ⚪
* Instantly flattens a **3x3 area** using dirt from your inventory.

### 🥽 Obsidian Goggles 🟣
* Allows looking at Endermen without angering them.
</details>

## ✨ Potions & Effects 🥛
* **Phasing:** Walk through walls (max 1 block thick).
* **Long Arms (Reach):** Increases interaction range by **+2.0 blocks**.
